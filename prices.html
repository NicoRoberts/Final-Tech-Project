Prices

Cabin

The total price to stay in the 400 square foot cabin is approximately 100 dollars a night plus tax during the months of July, August, and September. All other months are available by month rent only at the price of 1,200 dollars. If you choose to stay here during the Summer, our handiman, Nico, will set you up with all equipment needed for fishing, activities, filleting, and packaging. Please reserve accordingly.

House
