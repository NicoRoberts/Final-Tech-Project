Kenai River Keystone Retreat

34335 Keystone Dr, Soldotna, AK 99669

5 beds 3 baths 2,300 sqft

Live an Alaskan adventure on the world-famous Kenai River! Gorgeous log home, warm and homey with open floor plan. Only 20 steps from awesome Alaskan salmon runs (Sockeye, King, Silver) as well as Rainbow Trout, Dolly Varden and other species. The property is engulfed by nature (both wildlife and scenery) which makes this an extra enjoyable place to live, yet a big bonus is that the property is only 2.5 miles to the best services and conveniences on the Kenai Peninsula. The location is stunning ALL seasons of the year. When stay at a place dubbed “Keystone Retreat,” or just in Alaska in general, it’s pretty much a given that you’re in for a seriously awesome ride. The many communities that make up the 25,600 square miles of the Kenai Peninsula all have a few common denominators; incredible natural beauty, endless outdoor activities and some of the most genuine, kind-hearted people that you’ll ever meet in your lifetime. Couple those factors with a mild coastal climate and you’re in for the most picture-perfect Alaskan lifestyle. Warning: once you visit, you’ll never want to leave.

What I love about this home

The solemnity of the remoteness; yet the fact that the property is so close to town. The unrestricted access to the river with two launch ramps within two miles...gives freedom of having the boat in the water for entire summer season. The variety of wildlife and frequency of seeing the animals and eagles is the best "nature channel" you could ask for. Owning this riverfront property offers various living / rental opportunities which has allows you to meet wonderful people from around the world. The picnic and fireplace areas right near the house has produced wonderful memories for family and friends. Not only are the summertime activities great but winter time snowshoeing and cross country skiing right out your door is such a treat.
