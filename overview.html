Overview

Live an Alaskan adventure on the world-famous Kenai River! Gorgeous log home, warm and homey with open floor plan. Only 20 steps from awesome Alaskan salmon runs (Sockeye, King, Silver) as well as Rainbow Trout, Dolly Varden and other species. The property is engulfed by nature (both wildlife and scenery) which makes this an extra enjoyable place to live, yet a big bonus is that the property is only 2.5 miles to the best services and conveniences on the Kenai Peninsula. The location is stunning ALL seasons of the year.

MAIN HOUSE

Lower Level:

Main side entrance leads from deck to foyer and has a convenient built-in storage area for hats, coats, and gear. Main level has engineered hardwood floors and tongue & groove ceilings. Also has a full bath, guest room (w/ queen size murphy bed), gourmet kitchen with custom-cut granite countertops with top-end Electrolux appliances. Dining area and living room is illuminated by 2 stories of windows extending the roof peak. A free-standing wood stove, with large viewing glass door can heat you out of the house, even on the coldest of nights. Electrolux washer & dryer is located in the attached heated garage.

Upper Level:

Staircase leads to a landing which is open to the downstairs; great for a comfy sitting area, it is currently set up as a home office. The master suite is very large and fully carpeted. The bathroom sports a jetted tub, a newly remodeled ?zero clearance? walk in shower. Closets include walk-in & bi-fold double door closets. You can enjoy the wonderful mornings perched high above the river or watch the Northern Lights at night from the small balcony accessible through the double glass sliding door.

RENTAL / GUEST CABIN

The cozy cabin is enjoyed by all who stay in it and was improved to allow year round living. It can be listed on AirBnB during the summer fishing frenzy for $400 + night. Enjoy coffee by the free-standing wood stove while looking out the large sliding glass door which opens to nature, wildlife and the river! The cabin's loft space was doubled in size and has 2 bedrooms. The arctic entry was craftsman-built using beautiful post beam construction from locally sourced rough cut beams. Cabin main floor has "wood looking" waterproof flooring; the loft is fully carpeted. Instant hot water is enjoyable after a day out on the river. The cabin is heated via wood stove, a wifi controlled gas wall heater, and the arctic entry by radiant floor heat. Locally sourced birch was used for the cabinetry with some fun custom features like the arctic entry pass-through lighted wood storage cabinet with live-edge countertop. Currently the cabin is set up to sleep 7 (master bedroom queen, almost-new hideaway queen leather sofa, gorgeous queen oak sleigh bed and a twin mattress on the loft style bunk bed. The loft has large built in storage areas, a desk and a large leather comfy chair. PROPERTY FEATURES Kinetico water filtration system treats, purifies water with softener & sulfur guard to remove any odors plus a separate RO system services sink & fridge. Honda key start, backup generator stored in it's own custom-built ventilated shed. Storage shed has a custom-built lean-to for firewood dry storage. Fishing dock is custom-built for the property and is removed each season. Stainless steel sink fillet table with warm water supply allows for easy processing right at the river's edge. $1000's spent on bank revetment, complete with custom stairs installed to avoid foot traffic on the bank. Many established wild & domestic raspberry plants & rhubarb plants around the property. Gardeners will enjoy the fenced gardening area (to keep the moose out). There is an extra-large parking area for RV's, trailers or boats. TURN-KEY LIVING All furniture, electronics and even fishing gear is included. Totally ready for the next family to enjoy, all you need is your clothes!
